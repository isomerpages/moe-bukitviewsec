## In the News

#### Distinguished Chinese Language Teachers Award

Ms Charline Ang (fourth from right in photo) recently clinched the Distinguished Chinese Language Teacher Award, given in recognition of exemplary effort in teaching the Chinese Language. Ms Ang was chosen from a pool of more than 475 nominated teachers to emerge as one of the 8 to be granted this award.
