<h4>BACKGROUND</h4>
<p>The Youth Flying Club in Bukit View Secondary school, which was founded
in 2012, has fostered a strong partnership with Singapore Youth Flying
Club by being proactive and supportive towards all HQ-organised activities.
Through engaging in activities such as aviation knowledge, simulator flying
and aeromodelling, students have gained valuable insights into the world
of flying.</p>
<h4>OBJECTIVES</h4>
<p>The objectives of the Bukit View Secondary School's Youth Flying Club
are threefold:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To foster a deep interest in flying and aviation among club members.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To cultivate leadership skills among club members.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To assist in the character development of students who participate in
the clubâ€™s activities</p>
</li>
</ul>
