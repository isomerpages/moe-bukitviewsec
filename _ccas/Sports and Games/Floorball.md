<h2><strong>Floorball</strong></h2>
<h5><em>"A team above all. Above all a team."</em></h5>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/CCA%20Page/Group%20Photo/Floorball_Formal.jpg">
</div>
<h4>BACKGROUND</h4>
<p>Through teamwork, floorball aims to instill responsibility, confidence
and discipline in our students. The team is building up their skills and
strength through trainings and gaining experiences from tournaments and
friendlies</p>
<h4>MISSION</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>We aim to instill values that will help Floorball players become a responsible,
confident and disciplined team-player.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>We hope to increase people’s appreciation for Floorball and encourage
our students to stay healthy and active through sports while learning the
values of sportsmanship, leadership and zest.</p>
</li>
</ul>
