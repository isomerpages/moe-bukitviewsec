## Education Career Guidance

The ECG programme aims to help students discover and understand themselves so that students can decide on their future pathways. The programme aims to help students find answers to three important questions:  

 *   Who they are?
 *   Where they want to go?
 *   How do they get there?

**How is ECG carried out in schools?**

ECG is carried out through the following areas:  

 *   ECG Lessons and experiences
 *   Group and individual counselling

Examples of ECG experiences are:  

 *   Elective and Advanced Elective Modules
 *   Career Fairs
 *   Talks by Higher Institute of Learning
