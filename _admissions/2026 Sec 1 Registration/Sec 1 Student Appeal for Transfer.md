<h4>Sec 1 Student Appeal for Transfer</h4>
<hr>
<h3><strong>A. Appeals to Transfer Out of Bukit View Sec Sch (BVSS)</strong></h3>
<p><em>(Serious Medical Grounds)</em>
<br>For students <strong>posted to Bukit View Secondary School (BVSS)</strong> who
are seeking a transfer out due to <strong>serious medical conditions</strong> (e.g.
chronic heart conditions) or <strong>severe physical impairments</strong> (e.g.
requiring permanent use of a wheelchair), parents may submit an appeal <strong>through the posted secondary school</strong>.</p>
<h3>Appeal Deadline</h3>
<p>Appeals must be submitted by <strong>12.00 pm, Monday, 22 December 2025</strong>.
<br>
