<h4><strong>For students requesting to transfer out of BVSS (due to serious medical conditions)</strong></h4>
<p>For students posted to BVSS but are seeking a transfer out due to <strong>serious medical conditions (e.g. chronic heart conditions) or severe physical impairments (e.g. require permanent use of a wheelchair)</strong>,
parents can<strong> file their appeals</strong> at the<strong> posted secondary school by 12pm, 22 December 2025.</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Required Documents</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>For students with <strong>serious medical condition</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>An official letter or medical report from a certified doctor must be attached
to the application.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>For students with <strong>severe physical impairments</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Medical report clearly describing:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The extent of the impairment (temporary/permanent).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The assistive device currently in use (e.g., wheelchair).</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Please bring along the relevant medical documents (stated above) to support
your appeal for school transfer. For further clarification, please contact
the school’s General Office at 6566 1990.</p>
<p>For more information, you may also refer to <strong><a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/secondary/s1-posting/results/appeal-for-school-transfer</a></strong>
</p>

<h4><strong>For students requesting to transfer into BVSS</strong></h4>
<p>Our transfer appeal period is open until <strong>Friday, 26 December 2025, 12:00 PM.</strong>
</p>
<p>You do not need to come in person and may submit your appeal via this
link:
<br><a href="https://form.gov.sg/6756641117ccd26f81e611d6" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">2026 BVSS Sec 1 Appeal </a>
<br>
</p>
<p>The school will contact parents whose appeals are successful by <strong>2 January 2026</strong>.</p>
<p>Please note that students who wish to appeal for a school transfer may
submit their appeals but are likely to be unsuccessful if they have not
met the cut-off point for the respective courses in this year’s Secondary
One Posting.</p>
<p>All applications are reviewed <strong>carefully and holistically</strong>,
taking into consideration factors such as the student’s <strong>academic profile</strong>, <strong>interests and passions</strong>,
and <strong>reasons for transfer</strong>.</p>
<p>Please note that admission is <strong>subject to the school’s admission criteria and availability of vacancies</strong>,
and submission of an application <strong>does not guarantee admission</strong>.</p>
<p>For the <strong>latest cut-off point (COP) information</strong> for the <strong>2026 Secondary 1 cohort</strong>,
please refer to <strong>MOE SchoolFinder</strong> via the link below:
<br><strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/schoolfinder-bvss" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/schoolfinder-bvss</a></strong>
</p>
